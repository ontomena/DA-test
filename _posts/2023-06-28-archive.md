For posts prior to June 2023, an archive site exists containing earlier iterations of this practice. The archive represents a different phase of work, before certain methods had fully crystallised.

Looking back through old projects reveals both continuity and rupture. Some concerns persist—walking, sound, text, displacement itself. But the approaches have shifted, become more deliberate perhaps, or at least more conscious of their own processes.

The archive remains accessible as a record, though not everything from that period merits preservation. Displacement operates on multiple timescales; some things need to be left behind to make space for what emerges next.
