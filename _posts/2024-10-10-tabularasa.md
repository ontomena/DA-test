An animated exploration of blank slates and fresh starts, combining visual movement with textual reflection and subtle soundscaping.

The piece examines the myth of the clean break, the supposed fresh start. Nothing is ever truly blank; traces remain, patterns persist beneath the surface. Through layered animation and fragmented text, the work reveals how displacement operates even in moments we imagine as new beginnings.

Sound design emphasises the residual, the remanent—what lingers after we think we've moved on.
