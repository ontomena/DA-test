An album exploring chains of consequence, tracing how small actions ripple outward into unexpected configurations.

The work developed from an interest in causal chains that become so attenuated they appear random. Each track follows a different trajectory—some linear, others recursive, a few that loop back on themselves to form strange attractors.

Recorded over several months in various locations, the pieces use both environmental recordings and instrumental interventions to map these hidden connections. The title refers both to political activism and the simple physics of cause and effect, suggesting they might not be as distinct as we imagine.
