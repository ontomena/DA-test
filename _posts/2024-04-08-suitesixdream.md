A six-part sonic journey through dreamscapes and liminal spaces, exploring the threshold between waking and sleeping consciousness.

Each movement drifts between tonal centres, never quite settling, mirroring the unstable geography of dream logic. Field recordings from nocturnal environments blend with processed instrumental fragments, creating a soundworld that hovers between the real and imagined.

The suite emerged from a period of disrupted sleep, where the boundaries between different states of awareness became increasingly porous. What began as documentation became composition.
