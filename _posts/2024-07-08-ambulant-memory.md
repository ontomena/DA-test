The same path walked twenty times over several years, each walk recorded. This piece layers fragments from different iterations, creating a composite acoustic portrait where past and present versions of the route coexist.

What emerges is less a documentary of place than an archaeology of repeated movement - how sound accumulates through ritual return, how landscape holds traces of its own observation. The ambulant becomes archive.
